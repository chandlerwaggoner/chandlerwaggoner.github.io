    Available Services

    Chandler offers a wide range of services perfect for whatever level of support you need in your child's university admissions journey. These include everything from one-off consultations, for those in need of just a little bit of advice, all the way through to full-service packages, working with your student on every step of the admissions process from exam prep through to the final admissions decisions. Chandler offers bespoke services depending on the level of your guidance and advising needs. 

    Initial Consultation

    In this 60-90 minute session, Chandler will sit down with you and your student to discuss your goals, your academic work up to this point, and your motivations for considering university in the USA. This session offers an introduction to the work required for applying to the USA. During this meeting, we will examine a number of university options for your student, and discuss what they should be focusing on as they move toward applying for admissions, looking at the full timeline of admissions one step at a time. This is also a chance for you to have any questions answered. Following this meeting, Chandler will provide you with a bespoke and clear list of colleges and universities to begin considering.

    Comprehensive

    Full Service Package

    Recognising the detailed and nuanced elements of applying to the USA for university, this package is an all-inclusive service in which Chandler will work directly with your student through every step of  the US admissions cycle, including:

    • Creating a Bespoke List of Universities with programmes geared toward your specific goals and interests

    • Moving step-by-step through the Common Application, providing guidance, advice, and more

    • Working through the complexities of the Common App essay by finding exciting and enticing stories to share

    • Honing in on the individual and specific questions of the individual university supplemental essay prompts

    • Offering extensive CV support, highlighting activities and honours/awards with precise and detailed descriptions

    • Assisting with alumni interview preparations and guidance

    Common Application

    Essay Support

    The Common App essay is no easy task, and for many students, it can be quite daunting. What can you write to be perceived as unique, passionate, intelligent, and focused, all within 650 words? With this service, Chandler will guide your student through the entire process of writing a dynamic and poignant essay sure to catch attention and be memorable.

    SAT / ACT

    Exam Tuition

    The SAT/ACT is a requisite and incredibly important part of the admissions process, and understanding how the exam is structured and built is even more vital to success on the test itself. Chandler approaches each student studying for these exams with a slightly different approach, recognising each comes with their own academic strengths and hurdles. Working with Chandler to prepare your student for the SAT / ACT will ensure they receive incredibly personalised and detailed care. Having guided countless students through these exams, he has a proven track record of success.

    Additional Services

    In addition to the services above, Chandler offers a wide variety of additional services, bringing his expertise to Arts/Drama/Music/Creative Writing supplements and applications.

    Drama School Applications / Auditions

    Chandler works closely to guide your acting student through auditions. Past acting students have gone on to receive recalls and offers from schools such as Juilliard, LAMDA, RADA, Guildhall, LIPA, Oxford School, and more. 

    Fine Arts Supplements / Applications 

    Chandler works directly with your student to curate and develop successful fine arts supplements. His students have gone on to gain admissions to schools such as NYU, UMich Stamps, Central St Martins, RISD, and more.

    Music School Portfolio / Auditions

    Chandler works with your music student directly, guiding them through the entire process of auditioning for music composition, performance, and tech programmes. Past students have gone on to Berklee and RAM.
